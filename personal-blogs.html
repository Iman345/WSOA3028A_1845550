Post One, The Queen of England is a lizard person

Tuesday, 1 January 1995 by Suki Strawberry

I would like to begin by clarifying that this is infact not a conspiracy theory but a cold hard fact. If you stare at the Queen of England, you will catch a glimpse of her reptilian eyes. The monarchy has always been suspected of having extra-terrestial communication but i think that's a rouse. Besides aliens may or may not be kind. However, the monarchy stands for anything but kindness. Lizard people are shape-shifting, blood thirsty creatures who wrongfully proside in places of power. They feed on human beings and are trying to form a colony on Earth so one day they may take-over. Multiple children have gone missing after visiting the palace as well as staff. There is currently no technology that can identify or defend against lizard people. They are most likely to be wealthy and rich and will definately be Capatilist. Rich people are weird to begin with so do yourself a favour and stay away from them anyway and if you are rich already, do not trust anyone. Beware of lizard people and keep your third-eye open.

Post Two, My fashion look of the week

Saturday, 07 January 1995 by Suki Strawberry

This is my outfit of the week. Inspired by Wednesday Adams of the Adams family. I sewed the cape myself from a pattern in a knitting magazine. I found the magazine in my dentists office and borrowed the page from the magazine, if you wish to find the pattern I suggest you do the same. I wore this outfit to a poetry reading my dear and only friend Ken organised at a local coffee shop. It was painfully boring and did not speak to my soul. I hope everyone had a much more enlightening week. I noticed that no government agents followed me this week they must still be on holiday. Never forget they can always be watching so stay alert and keep your third-eye open.
