Post Six, My new years resolutions

Tuesday, 1 January 1995 by Suki Strawberry

Today is the first day of school, I mean university. Well it's my first day of my second year. I have a list of goals for this year.

  1. Make more friends. One friend min.
  2. Join a club that you did not start.
  3. Get people to join the clubs you did start.
  4. Prove at least three of my “conspiracy theories”.
  5. Try to go to a gym.
  6. Look after a plant.
  7. Try overalls.
  8. Go to a concert
  9. Do something illegal
  10. Learn to cook

I know some of my goals are unrealistic like number five and number three but it’s a new school year, maybe the force will work in my favour.
